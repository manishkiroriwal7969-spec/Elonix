Protocol Overview

The Blueprint of Hyper-Scarcity

ELONIX reimagines supply-side economics in decentralized finance. By hardcoding an absolute cap of just 182,500 tokens, ELX stands as one of the most hyper-scarce assets on the BNB Smart Chain network.

The emission protocol is simple, transparent, and completely automated. At deployment, 100% of the token supply was locked directly inside the smart contract code. Everyday, exactly 100 ELX tokens are unlocked and distributed to the mining wallet. This daily emission occurs exactly once every 24 hours over a strict 5-year cycle (1,825 days).

With zero transaction taxes, zero minting functions, and no capabilities for code upgrades or blacklisting, ELONIX sets the gold standard for trustless, mathematical decentralized design.
